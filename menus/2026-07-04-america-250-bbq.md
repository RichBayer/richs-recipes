# July 4, 2026 - America 250 BBQ Menu

## Occasion

Fourth of July and America's 250th anniversary.

## Main

- Pulled pork sandwiches
- Sweet Carolina vinegar sauce
- Soft buns
- Creamy slaw for topping or side
- Pickles

## Sides

- Baked beans
- Mac and cheese
- Potato salad or pasta salad
- Chips
- Watermelon or fruit tray

## Optional extras

- Deviled eggs
- Corn on the cob
- Lemonade
- Sweet tea
- Brownies
- Banana pudding
- Peach cobbler

## Abi's travel pork butt

Pack separately:

- Pulled pork in a covered foil pan
- Sweet Carolina vinegar sauce in a jar or bottle
- Extra buns
- Slaw in a separate container
- Serving tongs
- Foil

Travel note:

Keep warm if serving soon. Refrigerate if holding longer. Reheat covered with a splash of sauce or reserved pork juices.

## Serving notes

Sauce the pulled pork lightly before serving and keep extra sauce on the side. This keeps the pork flexible for people who like more vinegar bite or more sweetness.
