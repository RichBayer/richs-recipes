# Creamy Spinach & Artichoke Dip

## Overview

A rich, creamy baked spinach and artichoke dip for parties, BBQs, holidays, and game days. This version is built for flavor and texture: creamy base, plenty of cheese, drained spinach, chopped artichokes, garlic, and sweet onion.

Serve warm with sourdough bread, mini sweet peppers, tortilla chips, or crackers.

## Ingredients

- 2 (8 oz) blocks cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella
- 3/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan
- 1 (10 oz) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed very dry
- 1 (14 oz) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 small sweet onion, finely diced
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/4 tsp smoked paprika, optional

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Thaw the spinach and squeeze it very dry.
3. Drain and chop the artichoke hearts.
4. Sauté the sweet onion in olive oil until soft.
5. Add garlic and cook for about 30 seconds.
6. In a mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, sour cream, mayonnaise, mozzarella, Parmesan, salt, pepper, onion powder, and smoked paprika.
7. Fold in spinach, artichokes, and the onion-garlic mixture.
8. Spread into a baking dish.
9. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until hot and bubbling.
10. Let rest about 10 minutes before serving.

## Serving

Good dippers:

- Sliced sourdough bread
- Mini sweet peppers
- Tortilla chips
- Crackers

## Notes

- Squeezing the spinach dry is important so the dip does not turn watery.
- Letting the dip rest after baking helps it thicken before serving.
- For a more browned top, add a little extra mozzarella and Parmesan before baking.
