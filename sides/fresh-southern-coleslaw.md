# Fresh Southern Coleslaw

## Overview

A basic fresh Southern-style slaw for pulled pork sandwiches or as a BBQ side. Creamy, lightly sweet, tangy, and crisp.

## Ingredients

- 1 medium green cabbage, finely shredded
- 1/2 small purple cabbage, finely shredded, optional for color
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 3/4 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 2 tbsp granulated sugar
- 1 tsp celery seed
- 1/2 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper

## Method

1. Shred the cabbage and carrots.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, apple cider vinegar, sugar, celery seed, salt, and pepper.
3. Toss dressing with cabbage and carrots.
4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
5. Toss again before serving.

## Notes

- For pulled pork sandwiches, chop the cabbage a little finer so it sits better on the bun.
- Start with less dressing if making ahead, then add more before serving if needed.
- Best served cold and crisp.
