# House Pork Rub

## Style

A sweet and savory pork rub for smoked pork butt, ribs, and pork shoulder. This family version has no cayenne so the meat stays friendly for everyone, with heat available later through sauce.

## Batch for one pork butt

- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 tbsp paprika
- 2 tbsp kosher salt
- 2 tbsp coarse black pepper
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp onion powder
- 2 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp ground mustard powder

## Batch for two pork butts

- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup paprika
- 1/4 cup kosher salt
- 1/4 cup coarse black pepper
- 2 tbsp garlic powder
- 2 tbsp onion powder
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 2 tsp ground mustard powder

## Notes

- Ground mustard powder adds savory depth, but this is not a mustard-sauce flavor.
- Skip cayenne for the family version.
- Add heat later with hot sauce or spicy BBQ sauce at serving.

## Use

1. Pat meat dry.
2. Apply a thin mustard binder.
3. Coat generously with rub.
4. Let the rub sit on the meat at least 30 minutes, or refrigerate overnight after injecting.
