# BBQ House Salad

## Overview

A simple no-tomato salad for BBQ meals. Crisp iceberg, baby spinach, sharp cheddar, chickpeas, olives, carrots, and cucumber.

## Ingredients

- Iceberg lettuce, chopped
- Baby spinach
- Shredded sharp cheddar cheese
- Chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- Sliced green olives
- Shredded carrots
- Cucumber slices

## Method

1. Chop iceberg lettuce.
2. Add baby spinach.
3. Top with shredded sharp cheddar, chickpeas, sliced green olives, shredded carrots, and cucumber slices.
4. Serve cold with dressing on the side.

## Notes

- No tomatoes.
- Good dressing options: ranch, Italian, vinaigrette, or blue cheese.
- Keep toppings separate until close to serving if making ahead.
