# Sweet Carolina Vinegar Sauce

## Style

Carolina-style vinegar sauce with a little sweetness. Not mustard-based.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups apple cider vinegar
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 1 tbsp hot sauce
- 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
- 2 tsp kosher salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder

## Method

1. Add all ingredients to a small saucepan.
2. Simmer for 5-10 minutes, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
3. Cool before bottling.
4. Taste after cooling and adjust.

## Adjustments

- Sweeter: add more brown sugar.
- Tangier: add more apple cider vinegar.
- Spicier: add more hot sauce or red pepper flakes.
- Thicker/redder: add a little more ketchup.

## Use

Use lightly when pulling pork, then serve more on the side.

For travel pans, sauce the pork lightly and send extra sauce separately so the meat does not get soggy.
