# July 4, 2026 - America 250 BBQ Menu

## Occasion

Fourth of July and America's 250th anniversary.

## Main

- Smoked pulled pork sandwiches
- Soft buns
- Sweet BBQ sauce on the side
- Carolina vinegar sauce on the side
- Hot sauce on the side

## Sides and appetizers

- Fresh Southern coleslaw
- Creamy spinach and artichoke dip
- Mini sweet peppers for dipping
- Sliced sourdough bread for dipping

## Salad

No tomatoes.

- Iceberg lettuce
- Baby spinach
- Shredded sharp cheddar
- Chickpeas
- Sliced green olives
- Shredded carrots
- Cucumber slices

## Abi's travel pork butt

Pack separately:

- Pulled pork in a covered foil pan
- Sweet BBQ sauce in a bottle or jar
- Carolina vinegar sauce in a bottle or jar
- Extra buns
- Slaw in a separate container
- Serving tongs
- Foil

Travel note:

Keep warm if serving soon. Refrigerate if holding longer. Reheat covered with a splash of sauce or reserved pork juices.

## Serving notes

Keep the pulled pork flexible. Pull it close to serving time and moisten lightly with reserved pork juices if needed. Serve sauces on the side so people can choose sweet BBQ, vinegar sauce, hot sauce, or no sauce.
