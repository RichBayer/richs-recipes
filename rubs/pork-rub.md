# House Pork Rub

## Style

A simple, slightly sweet pork rub for smoked pork butt, ribs, and pork shoulder.

## Batch for two pork butts

- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/4 cup paprika
- 3 tbsp kosher salt
- 3 tbsp coarse black pepper
- 2 tbsp garlic powder
- 2 tbsp onion powder
- 1 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cayenne, optional
- 1 tsp ground mustard powder, optional

## Notes

Ground mustard powder is optional. Skip it for a no-mustard flavor profile.

## Use

1. Pat meat dry.
2. Apply a light binder.
3. Coat generously with rub.
4. Let the rub sit on the meat at least 30 minutes, or refrigerate overnight.
